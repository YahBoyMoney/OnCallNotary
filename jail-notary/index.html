Common Documents We Notarize at Jails

We handle a wide range of notary services for inmates, with a focus on legal accuracy and confidentiality.

Power of Attorney

We notarize financial, medical, and general power of attorney documents allowing an inmate to designate someone to handle affairs while incarcerated.

Child Custody Documents

Temporary guardianship forms, custody agreements, and parental consent documents for children's care while a parent is in custody.

Vehicle Release Forms

Notarized authorization to release an impounded vehicle or transfer ownership of a vehicle while the owner is incarcerated.

Real Estate Documents

Deeds, mortgage documents, property transfers, and lease agreements requiring the inmate's notarized signature.

Legal Affidavits

Sworn statements, court documents, settlement agreements, and other legal papers requiring notarization while in custody.

Marriage Documents

Marriage license applications, affidavits of inability to appear, and other documents for inmates getting married while incarcerated.
