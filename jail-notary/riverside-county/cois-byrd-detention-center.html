Cois M. Byrd Detention Center Information

Cois M. Byrd Detention Center, formerly known as Southwest Detention Center, is located in Murrieta, serving Southwest Riverside County.

Facility Location

Address: 30755-B Auld Road, Murrieta, CA 92563

Phone: 951-696-3050 (General facility line)

The Cois Byrd Detention Center is a large jail facility that handles arrestees from the southwest region (Temecula, Murrieta, etc.) and holds both pre-trial inmates and those serving county jail sentences.

Visiting Appointments

To visit an inmate at Cois M. Byrd Detention Center, you must call 951-341-8888 (the same central scheduling line for all Riverside County jails) between 9:00 AM and 5:00 PM to book a visit at least one day prior.

Visiting days at the Murrieta jail typically include weekends and a couple of weekdays; exact schedules can be found on the sheriff's website or by phone. Each visit is around 45 minutes, and inmates are allowed a limited number of visits weekly.

Murrieta's facility has modern visiting booths with telephones (for non-contact visits).

Visitor Rules:

All visitors must be 18 or older or accompanied by a parent/guardian. A valid government ID is required. The dress code is strictly enforced (no shorts above mid-thigh, no see-through clothing, etc., similar to the rules at RPDC).

Lockers may be available to store keys or small items during your visit.

Inmate Search & Mail

Use the Riverside County online inmate locator to confirm if your loved one is at Cois M. Byrd Detention Center (search by name, and the system will indicate "Southwest Detention Center" or "Cois Byrd Detention" as the location).

Mailing Address:

Inmates here receive mail at the facility's street address. According to official info, address mail as: (Inmate Name & Booking Number), Cois M. Byrd Detention Center, 30755-B Auld Road, Murrieta, CA 92563.

(The "-B" in the street address is part of the official address.) Ensure the booking number is included for proper delivery.

Documents We Notarize at Cois Byrd Detention Center

Common documents we notarize for inmates at Cois Byrd include:

Real Estate Documents

Southwest Riverside County is a growing area; if an inmate homeowner needs to notarize a deed, refinance documents, or a permission for sale of property, we handle those. We've notarized documents for inmates dealing with property sales so their family isn't stuck waiting for their release to complete escrow.

Family Law Documents

Inmates sometimes have ongoing family court matters. We notarize waivers, stipulations, or custody agreements that an inmate needs to sign for family court. For example, an inmate parent might notarize an agreement granting full custody to the other parent or approving a stepparent adoption.

Military or VA Forms

Murrieta is near military installations (like Camp Pendleton is not far). If an inmate is a veteran or service member, they may need to sign Veterans Administration or military paperwork (like benefits proxy or GI Bill benefit assignments). We can notarize those forms to help maintain their benefits or appointments.

General Powers of Attorney

We prepare and notarize general POAs for inmates who trust someone to handle all personal matters while they are incarcerated. This can cover banking, insurance claims, car registrations, etc. We make sure the power of attorney language meets California's requirements and is accepted by the institutions where it will be used.

Financial Documents

For instance, if an inmate co-owns a business or is on the board of an organization, resolutions or agreements might need their notarized signature (even things like refinancing a business loan, etc. can come up). We've notarized documents like corporate resolutions at this facility when needed.

Child Custody Agreements

Documents establishing temporary guardianship or custody arrangements for children while a parent is incarcerated. These documents need proper notarization to be legally valid for schools, medical facilities, and other institutions.
