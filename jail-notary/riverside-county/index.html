Riverside County Jail Notary Services

We provide professional notary services at all Riverside County detention facilities, ensuring your documents are properly executed even when your loved one is incarcerated.

Riverside County operates multiple detention facilities throughout the county, from Riverside city to the Coachella Valley. Each facility has specific protocols for notary services, and our experienced team is familiar with all of them. We work efficiently with jail staff to ensure a smooth process for document notarization.

Our mobile notary service travels to all Riverside County detention centers, providing professional notarization for various documents including powers of attorney, real estate documents, custody arrangements, and more. We understand the urgency often associated with these documents and strive to schedule appointments promptly.

All of our notaries are commissioned by the State of California and maintain thorough records of all notarizations in compliance with state regulations. We bring all necessary supplies, including our official seal, journal, and pens, ensuring a complete and legally valid notarization every time.

Common Documents We Notarize at Riverside County Jails

Our mobile notaries are experienced in handling various document types for inmates:

Powers of Attorney

General, limited, and durable powers of attorney allowing designated individuals to handle specific matters during incarceration.

Real Estate Documents

Deeds, mortgage documents, refinancing agreements, and property transfers requiring notarized signatures.

Child Custody Documents

Temporary guardianship forms, custody agreements, and consent forms for minor children during parent incarceration.

Business Documents

Business ownership transfers, corporate resolutions, partnership agreements, and other business-related documents.

Financial Authorizations

Bank account access authorizations, financial power of attorney forms, and other financial management documents.

Legal Affidavits

Sworn statements and affidavits for legal proceedings, including those related to the inmate's case or other civil matters.

Our Riverside County Jail Notary Process

We've simplified the process of getting documents notarized for inmates:

1

Initial Consultation

Call us at (909) 751-8439 to discuss your specific needs and the facility where your loved one is located. We'll explain the process and any facility-specific requirements.

2

Document Preparation

We'll help you understand what documents are needed and how to prepare them. For many document types, we can provide templates or guidance on proper formatting.

3

Scheduling

We'll coordinate with the detention facility to schedule an appointment for the notarization, working within their visitation protocols and hours.

4

On-Site Notarization

Our notary will meet with the inmate at the scheduled time, verify their identity, and complete the notarization process in compliance with California notary law.

5

Document Delivery

After notarization, we can either provide the documents to a designated family member, arrange for secure delivery, or handle filing if required.
