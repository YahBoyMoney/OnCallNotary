How much does a mobile notary cost in San Bernardino?

Our mobile notary services in San Bernardino cost $15 per signature (California's maximum statutory fee) plus a travel fee based on your location. For residential and business locations, the travel fee varies depending on distance. Please note that calls made outside of regular business hours will incur a higher travel fee. Contact us for a specific quote based on your location and timing needs.

What areas do you serve?

We provide mobile notary services throughout San Bernardino County including San Bernardino, Colton, Rialto, Highland, Redlands, Riverside, Fontana, Ontario, and Rancho Cucamonga. We also serve surrounding areas in the Inland Empire. If you're unsure if we serve your area, please contact us.

What documents can a mobile notary in San Bernardino notarize?

Our San Bernardino mobile notaries can notarize a wide range of documents including real estate transactions, powers of attorney, medical directives, loan documents, affidavits, trust documents, vehicle transfers, and more. If you're unsure whether your document requires notarization, contact us and we'll be happy to assist.

What ID is required for notary services in California?

California law requires valid government-issued photo identification for notarization. Acceptable forms include a California driver's license or ID card, U.S. passport, military ID, or other government-issued ID. The ID must be current (not expired) and must include a photograph, physical description, signature, and an identifying number. All signers must have their ID and paperwork ready when the notary arrives to ensure an efficient process.

What should I prepare before the notary arrives?

Before your scheduled appointment, please have the following ready: 1) All documents that need to be notarized (unsigned), 2) Valid government-issued photo ID for each person requiring notarization, 3) Any supporting documents mentioned in your paperwork, and 4) Payment method. Having everything prepared will help make the notarization process quick and efficient.
