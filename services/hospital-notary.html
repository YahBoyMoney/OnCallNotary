Hospital Notary Services

We provide compassionate, urgent mobile notary services for patients and families in hospitals, nursing homes, and medical facilities throughout San Bernardino County.

Call Now: (909) 751-8439

What We Notarize

  • Advance healthcare directives
  • Medical power of attorney
  • Living wills
  • Other time-sensitive documents

We understand the urgency and sensitivity of hospital notarizations. Our mobile notaries are available 24/7 and can come directly to your facility, often within hours.
