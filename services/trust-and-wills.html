Frequently Asked Questions About Trust & Wills Notarization

Do I need witnesses for trust or will notarization?

Some estate documents require witnesses in addition to a notary. We can advise you on requirements and help coordinate witnesses if needed.

What do I need to bring for notarizing a trust or will?

You'll need a valid government-issued photo ID and the unsigned documents. We'll walk you through the process.

Can you come to a hospital or care facility?

Yes, we frequently provide notary services for trusts and wills at hospitals, care homes, and assisted living facilities.
